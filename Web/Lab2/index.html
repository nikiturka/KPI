Haikyuu!!

"Haikyuu!!" is a popular Japanese anime and manga series centered around a high school volleyball team. The anime tells the story of Shoyo Hinata, an exceptionally talented volleyball player who is of short stature but possesses a strong determination to become a true volleyball player despite his physical limitations. He joins his old school's volleyball team and does everything in his power to make a name for himself on the court and elevate his team to a high level.

Haikyu!! is coming to an end with a two movie project taking on the last half of Haruichi Furudate's original manga, and the first Haikyu!! movie coming our way has set its first release date with its first trailer and poster! Haikyu!! ended its TV anime run with four seasons under its belt, and while fans had suspected it would be returning for Season 5 someday, it was surprisingly announced instead that the anime would be coming to an end with two new movies planned as part of the Haikyu!! FINAL project. The first of which is hitting theaters across all the world starting in 2024.
